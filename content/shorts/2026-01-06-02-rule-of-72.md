You have ₹10,000 available, but you are split between spending it, saving it, starting a SIP, or keeping it for emergencies.

Rule of 72 helps you give money a purpose instead of reacting emotionally to every choice.

If you receive ₹10,000, you might split it between emergency cash, a SIP, and a goal-based bucket. The point is not excitement; it is direction.

5. Key Takeaway
Use rule of 72 as a decision filter, not as a shortcut.

6. CTA
Share this with someone learning money basics.
