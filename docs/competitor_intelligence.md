# Competitor Intelligence

Competitor intelligence is stored locally in:

```text
market_intelligence/competitors.csv
```

Tracked fields:

- Channel Name
- Content Category
- Top Performing Topics
- Posting Frequency
- Notes

## Categories

- Wealth Building
- Trading Basics
- Investor Toolkit
- Market Vocabulary
- Behavioral Finance

## Usage

Use competitor intelligence to identify:

- Repeated educational formats
- Topic demand signals
- Posting cadence norms
- Content gaps in the UNLOAN calendar

Do not use it to copy scripts, thumbnails, brand language, claims, comments, or engagement tactics.

## Compliance

Competitor analysis must remain educational and strategic. It must not become:

- Automated engagement
- Comment automation
- Scraping for reposting
- Stock recommendation generation
- Platform policy circumvention
