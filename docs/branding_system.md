# Branding System

UNLOAN Media brand assets live in:

```text
assets/colors.json
assets/typography.json
assets/branding.json
assets/logo_guidelines.md
assets/logo.png
```

Use `assets/logo.png` as the default logo for all UNLOAN Media branding and content packages.

## Brand Voice

- Simple
- Calm
- Beginner friendly
- Educational
- Risk aware

## Category Visual Systems

- Wealth Building: emerald accents and growth visuals.
- Trading Basics: amber accents and risk-control visuals.
- Investor Toolkit: cyan accents and tool/interface visuals.
- Behavioral Finance: violet accents and decision psychology visuals.
- Market Vocabulary: gold accents and definition-card visuals.

## Typography

Use large mobile-readable headlines and clean sans-serif body text.

Avoid cramped text, tiny disclaimers, and decorative fonts that reduce clarity.

## Logo Use

Follow `assets/logo_guidelines.md`.

Do not imply approval, partnership, publishing, or authentication with Instagram, YouTube, Canva, Meta, Google, or third-party platforms.
