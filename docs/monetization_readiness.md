# Monetization Readiness

Phase 6 prepares a local monetization dashboard:

```text
data/reports/monetization_dashboard.json
```

Tracked metrics:

- Followers
- Subscribers
- Views
- Watch time
- Engagement
- Engagement rate

## Readiness Levels

Foundation:
Audience and content system are being built. Focus on consistency, clarity, and educational trust.

Early Traction:
Content is showing repeatable viewership. Focus on series formats, audience retention, and higher save/share rates.

Scaling:
Audience and watch-time signals are strong enough to prepare sponsorship, partnership, membership, or product-readiness reviews.

## Rules

- Monetization must not depend on stock tips or buy/sell calls.
- Do not promise returns.
- Do not imply guaranteed outcomes.
- Keep all content beginner-friendly and educational.
- Validate any future monetization offer separately for compliance.
