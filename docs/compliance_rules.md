# Compliance Rules

UNLOAN Media content is educational only.

Every script must:

- Avoid stock recommendations.
- Avoid buy or sell recommendations.
- Avoid return guarantees.
- Avoid profit claims.
- Avoid implying trading is easy, safe, or assured income.
- Include the disclaimer: `Educational content only. This is not financial advice.`

## Allowed

- Beginner explanations of investing terms.
- General education about risk, discipline, and market concepts.
- Plain-English examples that do not name a stock as an opportunity.
- Calls to save, share, comment, or follow.

## Not Allowed

- Specific stock tips.
- Entry, exit, or target price recommendations.
- Guaranteed-return language.
- Claims that a strategy will make money.
- Personalized financial advice.
- External publishing, scheduling, authentication, or API integration.

## Review Checklist

Before publishing outside this repository workflow, confirm:

- The topic matches the approved calendar.
- The script uses simple English.
- No stock, product, or trading setup is presented as a recommendation.
- The disclaimer is present.
- The CTA is educational and not misleading.
