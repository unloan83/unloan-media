# Storyboard: CAGR

Category: Investor Terminology
Brand Tagline: Build Wealth. Not Debt.
Audience: Young Investors (20-35)
Logo: assets/logos/logo.png

Scene 1:
Hook: This one number can look smart, but mislead beginners fast.

Scene 2:
Problem: You see cAGR on an app, a post, or a portfolio screenshot, but you are not sure whether it actually helps your decision.

Scene 3:
Explanation: CAGR is a lens for understanding a business or market idea, not a green signal to buy anything.

Scene 4:
Example: If two companies are in your watchlist, do not use one metric alone. Combine it with debt, growth, cash flow, and your overall portfolio risk.

Scene 5:
Takeaway: Use cAGR as a decision filter, not as a shortcut.

Scene 6:
CTA + Logo: Save this before reading any return chart. Use assets/logos/logo.png exactly as supplied. Add: Educational content only. This is not financial advice.
