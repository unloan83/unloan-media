This one number can look smart, but mislead beginners fast.

You see ePS on an app, a post, or a portfolio screenshot, but you are not sure whether it actually helps your decision.

Simple explanation: EPS is a lens for understanding a business or market idea, not a green signal to buy anything.

Example: If two companies are in your watchlist, do not use one metric alone. Combine it with debt, growth, cash flow, and your overall portfolio risk.

Use ePS as a decision filter, not as a shortcut.
