# Storyboard: Dividend

Category: Investor Terminology
Brand Tagline: Build Wealth. Not Debt.
Audience: Young Investors (20-35)
Logo: assets/logos/logo.png

Scene 1:
Hook: Before you buy your first stock, know what this term is really telling you.

Scene 2:
Problem: You see dividend on an app, a post, or a portfolio screenshot, but you are not sure whether it actually helps your decision.

Scene 3:
Explanation: Dividend is a lens for understanding a business or market idea, not a green signal to buy anything.

Scene 4:
Example: If two companies are in your watchlist, do not use one metric alone. Combine it with debt, growth, cash flow, and your overall portfolio risk.

Scene 5:
Takeaway: Use dividend as a decision filter, not as a shortcut.
