Before you buy your first stock, know what this term is really telling you.

You see dividend on an app, a post, or a portfolio screenshot, but you are not sure whether it actually helps your decision.

Simple explanation: Dividend is a lens for understanding a business or market idea, not a green signal to buy anything.

Example: If two companies are in your watchlist, do not use one metric alone. Combine it with debt, growth, cash flow, and your overall portfolio risk.

Use dividend as a decision filter, not as a shortcut.
