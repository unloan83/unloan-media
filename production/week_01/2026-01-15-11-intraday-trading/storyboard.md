# Storyboard: Intraday Trading

Category: Trading Basics
Brand Tagline: Build Wealth. Not Debt.
Audience: Young Investors (20-35)
Logo: assets/logos/logo.png

Scene 1:
Hook: Most trading mistakes start before the trade is placed.

Scene 2:
Problem: A young investor sees a fast-moving price, enters quickly, and only later asks, "How much could I lose here?"

Scene 3:
Explanation: Intraday Trading is about deciding the risk before thinking about the possible reward.

Scene 4:
Example: If a ₹10,000 trade can lose ₹500, ask whether the possible reward is worth that risk before entering. If the answer is unclear, the trade is unclear.

Scene 5:
Takeaway: Use intraday Trading as a decision filter, not as a shortcut.
