On-screen text: Swing Trading: Learn It Simply

2. Real-Life Problem
Voiceover: A young investor sees a fast-moving price, enters quickly, and only later asks, "How much could I lose here?"

3. Simple Explanation
Voiceover: Swing Trading is about deciding the risk before thinking about the possible reward.
