# Storyboard: Inflation

Category: Wealth Building
Brand Tagline: Build Wealth. Not Debt.
Audience: Young Investors (20-35)
Logo: assets/logos/logo.png

Scene 1:
Hook: What would you do differently if your next ₹10,000 had a clear job?

Scene 2:
Problem: You have ₹10,000 available, but you are split between spending it, saving it, starting a SIP, or keeping it for emergencies.

Scene 3:
Explanation: Inflation helps you give money a purpose instead of reacting emotionally to every choice.

Scene 4:
Example: If you receive ₹10,000, you might split it between emergency cash, a SIP, and a goal-based bucket. The point is not excitement; it is direction.

Scene 5:
Takeaway: Use inflation as a decision filter, not as a shortcut.
