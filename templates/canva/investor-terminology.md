# Canva Production Template: Investor Terminology

Format: 1080x1920 vertical video or carousel frame.
Brand Tagline: Build Wealth. Not Debt.
Audience: Young Investors (20-35)
Language: English

## Intro Screen

Open with a market term investors see often but may misunderstand.

## Content Screens

Use term cards, simple formulas, comparison panels, and one practical example.

## Outro Screen

Close with a one-line definition in plain English, CTA, disclaimer, and official logo.

## Logo Placement

Use assets/logos/logo.png exactly as supplied. Place it in the safe top-left or bottom-right area with clear padding. Do not crop, recolor, redesign, recreate, or substitute the logo.

## CTA Placement

Place CTA on the final screen and optionally as a small footer on content screens. Keep CTA short and mobile readable.

## Typography

- Headlines: Montserrat Bold.
- Body: Poppins Regular or Medium.
- No font substitutions in branded templates.

## Color Usage

- Primary: #0F172A
- Secondary: #FFFFFF
- Accent: #D4AF37
- Use high contrast and avoid decorative clutter.
