# Logo Guidelines

Use the UNLOAN name consistently as the primary brand signal.

## Placement

- Place the UNLOAN mark or wordmark in the top-left or bottom-right safe area.
- Keep enough margin so the mark does not touch screen edges.
- Do not place the logo over busy charts or high-contrast text.

## Clear Space

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to at least the height of the letter `U`.

## Color

- Prefer white logo text on navy backgrounds.
- Use navy logo text on light backgrounds.
- Do not use low-contrast logo colors.

## Usage Boundaries

- Do not combine the logo with Instagram, YouTube, Canva, Meta, or broker logos.
- Do not imply platform approval, partnership, or publishing status.
- Do not use the logo beside profit claims, return promises, or buy/sell signals.
