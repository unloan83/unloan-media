# Logo Guidelines

Use `assets/logo.svg` as the primary UNLOAN logo for all branding and content production.

The logo is a blue upward U-shaped arrow with subtle currency symbols and the UNLOAN wordmark.

## Placement

- Place the UNLOAN logo in the top-left or bottom-right safe area.
- Keep enough margin so the mark does not touch screen edges.
- Do not place the logo over busy charts or high-contrast text.

## Clear Space

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to at least the height of the letter `U`.

## Color

- Prefer the full-color logo on white or very light backgrounds.
- Use a white single-color version only if a dark video frame requires it.
- Do not recolor the full-color logo in a way that weakens the blue upward-arrow identity.

## Usage Boundaries

- Do not combine the logo with Instagram, YouTube, Canva, Meta, or broker logos.
- Do not imply platform approval, partnership, or publishing status.
- Do not use the logo beside profit claims, return promises, or buy/sell signals.
