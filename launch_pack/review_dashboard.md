## Required File Checklist

Each topic folder must contain:

- `reel_script.md`
- `short_script.md`
- `caption.txt`
- `hashtags.txt`
- `thumbnail.txt`
- `visual_brief.txt`
- `metadata.json`

## Pre-Publishing Review

- Confirm the reel script is clear within 30-45 seconds.
- Confirm the YouTube Short script is clear within 30-45 seconds.
- Confirm the caption is educational and does not include financial advice.
- Confirm hashtags are relevant and not misleading.
- Confirm the thumbnail title is short and phone-readable.
- Confirm the visual brief references `assets/logos/logo.png`.
- Confirm the logo is not modified in any way.
- Confirm no stock buy/sell recommendation is present.
- Confirm no profit claim or return guarantee is present.
- Confirm the educational disclaimer is present.
